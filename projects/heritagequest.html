Heritage Quest

Made in a weekend for Halifax Game Jam 2013.
Burnt Toast by Julia Tufts, most of the art by Kailey Mander.

CONTROLS/OBJECTIVES:

Baskets Back:

Get the baskets on either side of the court back before time runs out

WASD/Arrows = Movement
Shift = Punch
Control = Deploy/Remove Ladder
Space = Jump
B = Baskets Back

Burnt Toast:

Follow the clues as you navigate the brain maze to remove the correct brain chunk.

Mouse Only
